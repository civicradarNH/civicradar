                      About CivicRadar

                      Free community app to report civic hazards on a live ward map in Mumbai, Pune, and Thane. Not a government service or official complaint channel.

                      What you can do

                      • Report hazards with a photo pin — stagnant water, garbage, potholes, or broken streetlights
                      • Browse the ward map and tap Me too to corroborate nearby reports
                      • Get help filing with BMC, PMC, or TMC when you choose — after pinning on CivicRadar
                      • Track status, volunteer for cleanups, and see community progress on your ward

                      Who it's for

                      Residents, RWAs, and neighbourhood groups in Mumbai, Pune, and Thane — especially during monsoon when stagnant water and blocked drains matter most.

                      Privacy & data

                      Photo location metadata (EXIF) is stripped before upload. GPS is used only to place your pin when you allow it. Reports are visible to the community on the map. Official complaints go through BMC, PMC, or TMC when you file there.
