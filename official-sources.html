Official Government & Municipal Sources

Effective: 5 July 2026 · Last updated: 5 July 2026 · Operator: CivicRadar (operated by the project maintainers)

CivicRadar is NOT a government entity. CivicRadar is an independent community tool for neighbours in Mumbai, Pune, and Thane. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or operated by the Municipal Corporation of Greater Mumbai (MCGM/BMC), Pune Municipal Corporation (PMC), Thane Municipal Corporation (TMC), the Government of Maharashtra, or any government body. We do not submit complaints on your behalf and we do not publish government data as authoritative.

CivicRadar helps residents pin civic hazards on a community map and optionally track progress when you file with your local corporation. The links below are the official channels where citizens file and track municipal grievances. CivicRadar deep-links to these sources in-app; this page lists them in one place for transparency and app-store compliance.

How CivicRadar relates to official channels

  • Community map — user-generated reports visible to neighbours (separate from any government system).
  • Official filing — when you choose, use the verified links below to open BMC, PMC, TMC, or state portals and file there yourself.
  • No automatic filing — CivicRadar never sends your report to a corporation without you opening an official channel.

Official municipal sources

Mumbai — MCGM / BMC

  • BMC online complaint portal — https://portal.mcgm.gov.in/irj/portal/anonymous/qlcomplaintreg?guest_user=english
  • Participate Mumbai (civic engagement / volunteering — not complaint filing) — https://participatemumbai.mcgm.gov.in/
  • 24×7 BMC helpline — dial 1916
  • MyBMC WhatsApp — available via the MyBMC ecosystem (see in-app official channels)

Pune — PMC

  • PMC CARE portal — https://pmccare.in/cep/home
  • PMC CARE helpline — 1800 1030 222

Thane — TMC

  • TMC citizen portal — https://thanecity.gov.in/ (login → Online citizen services → File a complaint)
  • TMC helplines — 022-25331590 · 022-25331211 · citizen call centre 155300

Maharashtra & national programmes

  • Aaple Sarkar (Maharashtra grievance portal) — https://grievances.maharashtra.gov.in/en (select your local body when filing)
  • Swachhata-MoHUA (Swachh Bharat Mission ICT platform) — https://sbm.gov.in/sbmicc/ICT-platform · helpline 1969
